Help

How do I post?
Go to the Post page, enter your name and your thought, then click Post or press Enter.

How do I view posts?
Click Feed in the navbar to see all posts in order from newest to oldest.

How do I like a post?
Click the Like button on any post in the feed. You can only like each post once per session.

How do I delete a post?
Click the Delete button on any post. You will be asked to confirm before it is removed.

What are the formatting buttons?
On the post page, B wraps your selected text in bold markers, I wraps it in italic markers, and ❝ wraps it in quote marks.

How do I run this locally?
Open your terminal, navigate to the vardonit folder, run npm install then node server.js, and open http://localhost:3000.
